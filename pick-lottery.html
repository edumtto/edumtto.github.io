PickLottery

github.com/edumtto/pick-lottery

This personal project demonstrates my proficiency in leveraging the power of the CoreData framework in conjunction with the dynamic and sleek design capabilities of SwiftUI. The UI of this app has also been specifically designed and optimized to support the dark mode.

The project was crafted utilizing the following technologies:

  • SwiftUI for creating layouts and animations.
  • MVVM architecture.
  • XCTest for comprehensive unit testing.
