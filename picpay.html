Project Overview

PicPay, a widely-used digital wallet app in Brazil, was designed with millions of users in mind. Its primary focus is to provide convenience, user-friendliness, accessibility, and robust security, making it a trusted platform for everyday transactions and payments.

Key Features

Home Screen

Developed a dynamic banner system for quick and convenient remote configuration. Enhanced the suggestion list and implemented additional elements within the scroll view using the Texture framework, prioritizing performance.

Wallet Management

Redesigned the entire wallet feature, ensuring accessibility for screen readers. Implemented a credit/debit card manager for seamless management of payment methods.

Savings Account

Created a flexible savings account that allows users to organize their savings using virtual Piggy Banks. Involved in the project from inception, including design research and refinement.

Transaction Statement

Built a simplified statement screen that unified all transactions from different microservices within the app, providing users with a comprehensive overview of their activities.
