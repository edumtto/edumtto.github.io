PicPay

Payment App, Credit and Debit Card Manager, Investment Tools

PicPay, a widely-used digital wallet app in Brazil, was designed with millions of users in mind. Its primary focus is to provide convenience, user-friendliness, accessibility, and robust security, making it a trusted platform for everyday transactions and payments. To bring about large-scale features, I utilized the following technologies:

  • UIKit and programmatic layout for designing interfaces.
  • MVVM, Coordinator, and Modular architecture for flexibility and scalability.
  • Keychain for securely storing sensitive data.
  • Push notifications for real-time updates and engagement.
  • Camera integration for scanning QR Codes and Bar Codes.
  • Dispatch queues handling asynchronous flow and ensuring optimal performance.
  • REST API connections to facilitate communication with external services.
  • Analytics tools for monitoring usage and gathering insights.
